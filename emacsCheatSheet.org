* Standard Emacs
** Copy paste
*** Select - C-<spc>
*** Copy - M-w (Kill-ring-save)
*** Cut -  C-w (Kill)
*** Paste - C-y (Yank)

** Movement
*** Forward 1 char - C-f (forward)
*** Back 1 char - C-b (back)
*** Up 1 line - C-p (prev)
*** Down 1 line - C-n (next)
*** Forward 1 word - M-f
*** Back 1 word - M-b
*** End of file M->
*** Start of file M-<
** Undo C-x u
** Quit C-x C-c

** Set Bookmark   C-x r m
** GoTo Bookmarks C-x r b
** List Bookmarks C-x r l

** Minimize C-z

** Zoom in  C-x C-+
** Zoom Out C-x C--
